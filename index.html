Follow the instructions provided below to use our calculators.

  1. 1. Find the calculator that best matches your project type and use the caret to access it.
  2. 2. Enter the measurements of your project into the correct blank input fields. A visual aid is provided for reference.
  3. 3. Using the selection boxes choose a measurement type.
  4. 4. After all data is entered and measurements selected; click on the Calculate button.
  5. 5. Your answer will appear in the blank input field next to the label Total Yards.
  6. 6. If you make a mistake or want to enter new measurements click on the Reset button.
  7. 7. You can close the calculator by clicking on the caret . If you are on desktop simply move your cursor outside of the calculator menu.

All our calculators are designed to round up to the closest whole integer to ensure you always have enough concrete for your project.
